Movie Summary

Interstellar is a science-fiction film set in a future where Earth is slowly becoming uninhabitable. A group of astronauts travel through a wormhole in search of a new planet that can support human life.

Main Actors

The movie stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, a former NASA pilot turned farmer. Anne Hathaway plays Dr. Amelia Brand, a brilliant scientist on the space mission. Jessica Chastain and Mackenzie Foy portray Murph, Cooper’s daughter, at different stages of her life.
